Who we are

Moving people, not just seats.

RoadLine started with a simple frustration: booking an intercity bus shouldn't take longer than the trip itself. So we fixed it.

Our story

Eight years, one mission.

RoadLine launched in 2018 with three bus operators and a single route between two cities. Today, we work with hundreds of operators to put every seat, on every route, in one place — so riders spend less time comparing tabs and more time actually going somewhere.

We're not a bus company. We're the layer that makes finding, comparing, and holding a seat feel instant, transparent, and fair — whether you're booking a red-eye sleeper or a two-hour hop between towns.
